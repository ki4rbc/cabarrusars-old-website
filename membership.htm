Club Membership Dues

Club membership dues are the primary income for our amateur radio club.  As with any organization, we have operating expenses, so paying your dues annually is an important aspect in keeping our club financially healthy.  

Membership dues for a regular member cost $20 per year.  After paying for a regular membership, additional people in your family may become a club member for $7.50 each.  Click on the Constitution link above for more details about membership dues.

If you have NOT paid your annual membership dues and would like too, please see our club Treasurer at one of our monthly club meetings.  If you would like to pay them by mail, please send your check made out to "Cabarrus Amateur Radio Society" to the following U.S. Mailing address:

Cabarrus ARS
P.O. Box 785
Concord, NC 28026-0785
